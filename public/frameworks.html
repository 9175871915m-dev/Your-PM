Response Strategies

Avoid

Eliminate the threat by changing the plan

Transfer

Shift financial impact via insurance or contract

Mitigate

Reduce probability or impact through early action

Accept

Acknowledge and hold a contingency reserve

Optimised Response Checklist

  • ✓ Appropriate — scaled to significance
  • ✓ Cost-effective — value exceeds cost
  • ✓ Realistic — achievable in context
  • ✓ Agreed — stakeholder-approved
  • ✓ Owned — formally assigned

08 / Environment

PESTLE: External Factors

The value delivery system is influenced by external forces that can have favourable, unfavourable, or neutral impacts on project performance.

P

Political

Political climate, regional customs, governance structures, and trade policy.

E

Economic

Currency rates, interest, inflation, taxes, and tariffs affecting cost and investment.

S

Social

Demographics, public perception, ethics, and regional codes of conduct.

T

Technological

Market conditions, innovation trends, IP, and disruptive technology.

L

Legal

National laws on security, data protection, employment, and procurement.

E

Environmental

Physical working conditions, weather, ecological standards, and sustainability.

10 / Sustainability

The Triple Bottom Line

🌍

Planet

Green PM embeds environmental impact into project design from day one. Carbon footprint, material sourcing, and ecological risk are treated as first-class constraints alongside scope, time, and cost.

👥

People

Social impact — equitable labour, community consultation, and stakeholder wellbeing — is planned and measured. People are the project's most critical resource and most affected output.

📈

Profit

Long-term financial viability is evaluated alongside short-term returns. Sustainable value accounts for reputational risk, regulatory exposure, and full lifecycle cost.

Strategic Timing of Moves

First Mover

Enter first — build brand and competitive edge. Highest risk and cost.

Fast Follower

Rapidly adopt proven innovations. Lower risk, capture established market.

Late Mover

Enter established market. Focus on niche or benefit from stable technology.
